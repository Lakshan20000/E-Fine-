About E-Fine System

Transforming traffic fine management through digital innovation and service excellence

Our Story

Learn about our journey and commitment to modernizing traffic fine management

The E-Fine System was established in 2020 as an initiative of the Department of Traffic Police to revolutionize how traffic fines are managed and processed in the country. Born out of a need to reduce paperwork, improve efficiency, and enhance citizen convenience, our platform has grown to become the national standard for digital traffic fine management.

What began as a pilot project in the Colombo district has now expanded nationwide, serving millions of drivers with our secure, transparent, and user-friendly platform. Our team of dedicated professionals from both the technology and law enforcement sectors work tirelessly to ensure the system meets the highest standards of service and security.

Today, the E-Fine System processes thousands of transactions daily, helping maintain road safety while providing citizens with a fair and convenient way to address traffic violations.

Our Mission

To provide a transparent, efficient, and user-friendly digital platform that transforms traffic fine management, enhances road safety, and delivers exceptional service to all citizens while supporting law enforcement objectives.

Our Vision

To become the nation's most trusted and innovative digital platform for traffic law enforcement and driver services, setting the standard for transparency, efficiency, and citizen engagement in public service delivery.
